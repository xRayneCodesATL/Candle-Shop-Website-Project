SINsational Delights are carefully crafted, hand-poured, small batch, 100% natural soy wax candles. The scents are inspired by some of the greatest horror films, from Friday the 13th, to Halloween, we have them all!

As you may have noticed, our candles come in recycled containers. Every time you purchase a candle from us, we send you a return kit, in hopes that you will return your used candle. If you choose to do so, we will take 25% off of your next purchase just for helping us recycle!

We hope you're completely satisfied with your purchase. If by some chance our candle doesn't live up to your expectation, we will do everything in our power to make it right.

Thank you for supporting our dream by purchasing from our small, queer-owned business.

Love is Love,

About Us

ATL-based SINsational Delights is a queer BIPOC small-business. The idea of SINsational Delights stemmed from a shared love of horror and candles. Our hand-poured, small batch, 100% natural candles are inspired by the greatest horror films in history.

From John Carpenter's Halloween, to Wes Craven's Nightmare on Elm Street and everything in between, there is sure to be a candle crafted to suit all of your spooky-scented needs.

A little about the owners, Gem & Rayne... We've been together for 4 and a half years and have 2 beautiful kitties together. We wanted to build an affordable, high-quality brand with a warm and welcoming (and spooky) presence.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a little about who we are as a brand. We appreciate all of your support! Check back often as we're concocting new spells, I mean smells, frequently.
